Sri Bhagavathy Amman Cotton — operating as SBC Fabrics — is a premier knitted fabric store based in Tirupur, Tamil Nadu, India's garment capital. We specialise in supplying high-quality cotton and blended fabrics to garment manufacturers, exporters, and apparel brands.

From lightweight Single Jersey to heavy Loopknit and Interlock, our range covers every need with superior GSM consistency, rich colourways, and excellent finish.
